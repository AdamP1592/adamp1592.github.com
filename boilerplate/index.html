About Me

Hi, I'm Adam Peterson

I'm a passionate software engineer driven by innovation and a relentless pursuit of learning. I started programming at 14 because I saw it as a way to turn any idea into reality. Today, I focus on projects that push the boundaries of technology and explore the intersection of science and engineering.
My technical expertise spans Python and C++, with hands-on experience in Java, C, and full-stack web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript + jQuery). My toolkit also includes Docker, Kubernetes, Django, SQL and NoSQL databases, Git, Flask, and MongoDB. Beyond technical proficiency, I bring strong problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and adaptability—qualities honed by years of self-teaching and diving into scientific research.
I pride myself on my ability to learn independently and apply knowledge from diverse sources, from scientific papers on scalable pipelines for reconfigurable organisms to innovative neuro-simulation techniques. This continuous learning mindset is not just a skill for me—it’s a lifestyle that fuels my creativity and drive.
In addition to my technical and analytical abilities, my hands-on work as a garage door technician and carpenter has strengthened my communication, collaboration, and resilience. These roles have taught me to approach challenges pragmatically, work well under pressure, and value teamwork—qualities that are essential for any successful project.
Outside of work, I enjoy exploring the outdoors, which helps me recharge and gain new perspectives. I'm eager to collaborate on groundbreaking projects that blend technical innovation with creative problem-solving. If you'd like to connect or learn more about my work, feel free to reach out.
